Collaboration with Dr. J. Vondrak and Ivan Frolov

2014-10-01

Since the beginning of the project it has been clear that to understand the diversity in a complex group as is the genus Pyrenodesmia it we would need to sum up the efforts of most possible fellow researchers interested in this particular group within the Teloschistaceae. For this reason the collaboration established with Dr. Vondrak and I. Frolov (University of South Bohemia in České Budějovice) of is crucial, and has allowed us to have access to the plethora of material collected by them in eastern Eurasia and to join efforts with our molecular expertise to the delimitation of Pyrenodesmia under a wider geographic scope.

Collections

2014-09-13

During the summer of 2014 we focused in obtaining a collection of specimens of Pyrenodesmia to serve as main body for the study. Between April and Septemeber we collected several thousends of specimens of Pyrenodesmia as well as other calcicolous lichen species accross the western Mediterranean from Graz to Southern Spain at altitutedes ranging from Sea level to ca. 3000 m. The resulting dataset will serve as departing point for aour project butdue to its coverage will also serve as repository for further questions about the ecology and phylogeography of the group.
